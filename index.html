About ZBH Pan & Plate

Our Mission

ZBH Pan & Plate was created to bring the joy of cooking to everyone. Whether you're a beginner looking for easy weeknight dinners or an experienced cook seeking to master complex dishes, our curated collection of recipes has something for you.

What We Offer

  • Pakistani Cuisine: From aromatic biryanis and rich curries to sizzling kebabs and traditional stews.
  • Chinese Cuisine: Stir-fries, noodles, soups, and classic dishes with authentic flavours.
  • International Favorites: A growing collection of globally loved recipes.

Features

  • Detailed step-by-step instructions
  • Nutrition information for every recipe
  • Difficulty ratings and prep/cook times
  • Save your favorite recipes
  • Powerful search and filtering
  • Dark mode support

Technology

Built with vanilla HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript (ES6+). No frameworks, no dependencies — just clean, fast, and accessible code that works in any modern browser.
